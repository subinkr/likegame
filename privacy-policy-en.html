Privacy Policy

Like Game - Transform your life into an exciting game

1. Overview

Like Game (hereinafter "Company") considers the protection of users' personal information very important and complies with the Personal Information Protection Act to safely protect personal information.

This Privacy Policy applies to services provided by the Like Game app and website (https://likegame.life).

2. Personal Information We Collect

2.1 Required Information

  • Account Information: Email address, password
  • Profile Information: Nickname
  • Service Usage Information: Stat data, milestone progress, skill information, quest information

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device identifier, operating system information, app version
  • Service Usage Records: App usage time, feature usage status
  • Log Information: Access IP, access time, error logs

3. Purpose of Collection and Use of Personal Information

  • Service Provision: Account management, stat tracking, milestone management
  • Service Improvement: User experience enhancement, feature development
  • Customer Support: Inquiry response, technical support
  • Security: Prevention of unauthorized use, account protection

4. Retention and Use Period of Personal Information

Until membership withdrawal

Personal information is deleted immediately upon membership withdrawal, and is retained only for the period required by relevant laws and regulations.

5. Third-Party Provision of Personal Information

The Company does not provide users' personal information to third parties in principle. However, exceptions apply in the following cases:

  • When the user has given prior consent
  • When required by law or when requested by investigative agencies for investigation purposes in accordance with procedures and methods prescribed by law

6. Personal Information Processing Outsourcing

The Company outsources the following tasks to external specialized companies for service provision:

  • Supabase: Database hosting and authentication services
  • Vercel: Website hosting services

Outsourced companies take necessary measures for personal information protection and are obligated to comply with personal information protection obligations through outsourcing contracts.

7. Security Measures for Personal Information

  • Encryption: Personal information is encrypted for storage and transmission
  • Access Restriction: Access rights to personal information are minimized
  • Security Monitoring: Security monitoring and intrusion detection for personal information processing systems
  • Backup and Recovery: Regular backup and recovery system operation

8. User Rights and Obligations

8.1 User Rights

  • Request for access to personal information
  • Request for correction or deletion of personal information
  • Request for suspension of personal information processing
  • Request for transfer of personal information

8.2 User Obligations

  • Provide accurate personal information
  • Comply with precautions for personal information protection
  • Prohibit unauthorized collection of others' personal information

9. Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Company uses cookies on the website to enhance user experience. Cookies are used for the following purposes:

  • Maintain login status
  • Save user settings
  • Service usage statistics

You can refuse the use of cookies in browser settings, but some service usage may be restricted.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be changed in accordance with laws and policies, and users will be notified in advance through app notifications or email when changes are made.
